Our Story

At Café Amour, our story begins with a simple love for coffee — not just as a drink, but as a ritual, a reason to gather, and a spark for conversation. What started as a tiny corner café has grown into a cozy space where community thrives over freshly brewed espresso, flaky pastries, and heartfelt moments. We believe every cup tells a story — of passion, craft, and connection. Whether you're here for your morning pick-me-up or an unhurried afternoon escape, Café Amour is where warmth lives in every sip.
